About us

An unknown author once says “The best time for ice cream is always”. Precisely!

Craving's ice cream cafe opend its doors during February 2022,at the city of Thane In Maharashtra.It all started when we decided to launch a cafe near Thane station for refreshing travellers through our delicious ice creams.

OUR STORY

A friends group of seven dreamed of creating the most unbelievably delicious ice cream.Milk used for ice cream making obtain from our own dairy farm,which makes our ice cream more pure and hygienic.We believe all businesses should benifit people and community.That's why We are certified by global food safety for our purest milk.

How we make it good

We also provide vegan ice cream, our chocolate vegan ice cream take a lengthy and rewarding process to make. We make it over multiple days to get the best flavour and texture. The ice cream keeps well when stored in our freezer that has clean and continuous air flow.
